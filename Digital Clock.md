The clock is part 83910-12520
Pins from top to bottom
- **BAT (or BATT / BAT / sometimes “B+”)**  
    This is the **constant 12V battery power**. It keeps the clock running and preserves the time even when the car is off.
- **GND**  
    **Ground** connection (0V). This completes the circuit and is usually tied to the vehicle chassis.
- **LIG (likely “ILL” or “LIGHT”)**  
    This is the **illumination input**. It powers the clock’s backlight when you turn on the headlights or parking lights. Often tied into the dashboard lighting circuit.
- **ACC (Accessory)**  
    This is **switched 12V power** that turns on when the ignition is in ACC or ON. It tells the clock when the car is “active.”

Confirmed with multimeter

|     | Off | On  | Headlights |
| --- | --- | --- | ---------- |
| BAT | 12v | 12v | 12v        |
| GND | 0   | 0   | 0          |
| LIG | 0   | 0   | 12v        |
| ACC | 0   | 12v | 12v        |
